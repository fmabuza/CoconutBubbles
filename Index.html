    • Don't forget to Remember Me! 
    I miss your clean mouth and the way you taste.
What time did you wake up this morning? Have you 
had your coffee?

I dreamt you wrote me a love letter.
I never got to see your handwriting, how
do you hold your pen?

I almost went mad last week when your scent touched
my nostrils. I always knew you would be the death of me,
since the day you breathed on me.

Where are you? What are you doing?
Do you think of me? Do you miss me? Do you wonder
about what I’m wearing and what I’m feeling?

Do you hear a song and think about me? Do you meet people,
read something new, taste something different and wish you
could share with me?

I miss your arms around me. Only you knew how to hold me.
Last night was cold, how did you keep yourself warm?
The sunset had your favorite purple in it.

What am I gonna do if I never see you again?
I’m so scared of forgetting you, I don’t want to.
I don’t want to ever not feel about you. I won’t let myself.

What is your worst fear? Mine is that you will forget me
    .
